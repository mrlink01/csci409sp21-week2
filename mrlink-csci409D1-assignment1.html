About- Michael Link

My name is Mike Link and I am an information systems major from Colfax, North Carolina. My primary interests include API development and technical support. I decided to come to Coastal due to the degree-in-three program and the fantastic location. I am also a tutor for the computer science department and a teaching assistant for CSCI 140 and CSCI 150. Outside of class, I love to travel so I try to find somewhere new to visit every weekend. I'm also an avid golfer so I can often be found on the Hackler course here at Coastal.
